I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Software and Information Systems (SIS) in the College of Computing and Informatics at UNCC and co-advised by Dr. Bill Chu and Dr. Heather Lipford.

I completed my undergraduate studies in computer engineering at Isfahan University of Technology (IUT) in 2001. After about 10 years of work experience as a software developer and analyst, I completed my Master's in Information Technology  at University of Tarbiat Modares in 2013 before entering the PhD program at UNCC. 

My research interests include Software Security and Reliability, Data Mining, Visual Analytics and Fuzzy Systems.

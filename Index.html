I am a a PhD student researching software security in the Department of Software and Information Systems (SIS) in the College of Computing and Informatics at UNCC and co-advised by Dr. Bill Chu and Dr. Heather Lipford.

I received my BS in computer engineering at Isfahan University of Technology (IUT), Iran in 2001. After about 10 years of work experience as a software developer and analyst, I recieved my Masters in Information Technology  at University of Tarbiat Modares, Iran in 2013 before entering the PhD program at UNCC 2014. 

Research Interests :

My current research is focused on improving software security using program analysis of source codes in order to early detection of software flaws and vulnerabilities in development time. My other research interests include Formal Methods,Security Visualization, Data Security and Big Data.

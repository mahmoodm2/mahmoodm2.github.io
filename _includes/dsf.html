Mahmoud Mohammadi

I'm a PhD student at University of North Carolina at Charlotte. My research focuses on secruity unit testing which is to apply static/dynamic analysis to find security vulnerabilites in web applications.

I got my MS in informaition technoglogy in 2013 and my BS in computer engneering in 2001.

My research interesets are Software Security, Formal Methods, Data and Security Visualization, IoT Security
